## Claude-specific

- **Proactive board-keeping applies to you too.** AGENTS.md § "Task board
  (boardown)" is load-bearing for Claude Code, not just the "other agents" its
  header addresses. When you take on a substantive unit of work, log it on the
  `.boardown/` board (status `in-progress` → `done`) via the `boardown` CLI
  **without being asked** — don't wait for the owner to flag a missing task.
  Source of truth for the how/why stays in AGENTS.md; this is only a pointer.
  (Added after a session where the board went un-kept until the owner asked.)
